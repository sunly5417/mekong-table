How It Started

The Story Behind the Name

The Mekong River flows through six countries, connecting cultures, people, and traditions along its banks. When we opened Mekong Table in 2018, we wanted to capture that same spirit of connection — bringing together the deeply rooted culinary traditions of Cambodia with the beloved classics of Western cuisine.

Our founder, Chef Sunly Khloeng, trained in both Phnom Penh and Paris, returning home with a vision: a restaurant where a Cambodian family could enjoy Fish Amok alongside a table of travelers sharing their first Lok Lak — and where everyone felt equally at home.
